Canine (2023)

Short documentary written, produced & directed by Paul Webster. Winner of Audience Award for Best Short Film at the Cork International Film Festival 2023 and selected for Tribeca Film Festival 2024.

The life of an elite tracking dog as he is trained in Ireland then deployed to protect black rhino from ruthless poachers in Africa.

Cuan (2022)

An Cuan is a 4 x 1-hour long nature series that explores the Dublin Bay Biosphere. This is a unique urban area where nature and humanity at times live in harmony and at others battle to co-exist.

Written & Directed by Paul Webster.

The Vasectomy Doctor (2018)

Dr. Andrew Rynne was the first doctor to perform a vasectomy in Ireland. Rynne would continue to challenge the laws governing sexuality, laws imposed by Church and State. In 1990, a man walked into his surgery and shot him.

Written & Directed by Paul Webster.
